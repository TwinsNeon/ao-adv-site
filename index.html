MODERN NEON SIGNS

Modern neon light signs made from LED neon are safe, energy-efficient, and affordable. While traditional neon signs use glass tubes and neon gas, LED neon signs allow more people to enjoy a high-quality neon experience with a far greater range of options while costing significantly less.

Thanks to the affordability, efficiency, and durability of LED neon products, you can now take advantage of LED neon to customize signs, art and wall decor for a wide variety of uses. Add a stunning addition to your wedding decor, create a neon word sign for your wall, get a personalized man cave sign or a kid's room name light, add a unique touch with neon bar signs, a neon sculpture. Our customized light signs are suitable for any space.

TAKE IT OUTSIDE

WHEN YOU JUST CAN'T CHOOSE

Our customizable neon signs can be made in multiple LED neon colors, at no extra cost. Let our designers know what colors you want when you fill in the form. Or choose our RGB color-changing technology and change your sign's color, make it flash, pulse or flicker, brighter or dimmer at the touch of a button!

IT MOVED!

Our eye-catching animated signs are impossible to ignore. Specific parts of your custom sign can be programmed with up to 12 step animation. Create amazing signs by adding animation to a business logo or artwork like the custom LED neon signs we made for Coach.


ALL THE COLORS

UV printing is an effective way to add a sharp, vibrant colored image or text on your sign's backboard. It’s excellent for capturing finer details like gradients or elements of your design that may be too small for LED Neon. We can match any Pantone and CMYK codes.
